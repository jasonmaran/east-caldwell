The Bill of Commandments

  1. Thou shalt not questioneth ye olde government.
  2. Thou shalt not crosseth ye yonder border.
  3. Thou shalt not lieth with thy neighbor's wife.
  4. Thou shalt not espieth thy neighbor's wife through yonder window.
  5. Thou shalt not dreameth of thy neighbor's wife.
  6. Thou shalt not lieth with befurred beasts of the forest.
  7. Thou shalt not lieth with befeathered creatures of the sky.
  8. Thou shalt not lieth with bescaled creatures of the sea.
  9. Thou shalt not eateth thy neighbor's meat.
  10. Thou shalt not spitteth in thy neighbor's mead.
