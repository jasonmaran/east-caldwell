Important Events

The Great Crab Revolt of 1776

Furious with high taxes, the crabs attempted to overthrow the government. Fortunately, the men of East Caldwell put down the rebellion and enjoyed a magnificent Thanksgiving feast.

The Great Circus Tragedy of 1865

When an opium-addled clown accidentally freed Bimba, King of the Jungle, thousands lost their lives.

The Great Re-education of 1929

After the Great Crash, all radicals and reactionaries were rounded up and offered mandatory re-education. Those who did not accept re-education were fed to Bimba's offspring.

The Great Spider Invasion of 1952

Radioactive experiments created a race of giant, super-intelligent spiders. The arachnids kidnapped East Caldwell's women in hopes of creating a human-spider species. DDT bombs killed the spiders, but not before one hundred monstrous hybrids were born.
